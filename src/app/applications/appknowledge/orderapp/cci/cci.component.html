Central Customer Information(CCI)

CCI provides a superior method of creating and maintaining customer information within JB Hunt. The new application centralizes our customer information by replacing several customer creation and profile screens formerly used within the Business System, Host screens and comment fields throughout the company.  


CCI provides a central location to access customer information within the JB Hunt system. If the Customer Name is used as part of the search criteria, the system displays lists of possible location matches. After a search criteria is entered, the Search button is pressed to conduct an internal system search. The internal search enforces naming and address standards, decreases keying errors and prevents duplicate codes from being created. If no matches are found within our system, an extended search can be conducted.


  CCI provides access to an extended search for customer information outside JB Hunt. An extended search links the search criteria entered to the data stored by our Customer Information Service Provider. The extended search service allows us to validate new customer information before it is saved in our system. This service allows us to keep our customer information as current, standard and correct as possible.
