RATING AND BILLING (RBI)


Rating and Billing is an important application in pricing domain, we can rate and bill the load in RBI. Once the load got emptied in EOM th eload shows Estimated status in RBI and ready to rate and bill. we can also Rerated, Rebilled , Rerated Verify and voided the load . It also used to chcek the BTC, division, billing type , rating type, fleet code invoicing PDA and Post PDA Details


Once the Workobject created in filenet the load will pull in RBI. To Rate and bill the load ,RBI checks the CCI validation setup (Autorate, manualRate, Auto bill and Manual bill) and verify the papeworks are available . Load rates are pulling from Simonnow through Publication or spot price


Order flow will be seen in Billing order History tab. Original Invoice (Auxilary invoice) was created in Manual billing tab in RBI Once rated the statement invoices are shown in ESB application, it does not require the billing part .
